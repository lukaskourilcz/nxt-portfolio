# Portfolio — monetization

This is a personal freelance portfolio site. It is not monetized directly — its
job is to win work. Options below reflect effort-to-revenue for that goal.

| Option | Likelihood of income | Possible earnings | Pros | Cons |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Consulting / client lead funnel (the site's real job)** | Medium–High | project-based | Highest-value use; already the purpose | Indirect; depends on outreach + traffic |
| **GitHub Sponsors on linked OSS** | Low | $0–100/mo | Builds reputation; low effort | Small, slow, needs an audience |
| **Sell a portfolio template of this codebase** | Low | $0–300/mo one-off | Reuses the work | Support burden; crowded market |
| **Affiliate / referral links (tools you use)** | Low | $0–50/mo | Passive | Can cheapen a personal brand |
| Display ads | n/a | — | — | Wrong for a professional portfolio |

**Recommendation:** treat the site as a lead funnel for consulting; skip ads and
subscriptions. Sponsors/template only if an audience materializes.
